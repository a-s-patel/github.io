Budget Allocation Sheet

Balance proven channels with experiments while keeping spend within guardrails.

Use this sheet to map monthly budgets, identify reallocation opportunities, and document trade-offs.

  • Spend Guardrails

    Define max spend, efficiency targets, and acceptable CAC ranges.

    Input: Budget ceiling + ROAS floor

    Keep scaling decisions grounded in financial constraints.

  • Experiment Reserve

    Hold budget for testing new channels, audiences, or creatives.

    Rule: 10-20% test allocation

    Protect learning velocity without sacrificing core performance.

  • Scaling Lanes

    Separate channels into scale, maintain, and reduce categories.

    Signal: ROAS + CAC trend lines

    Avoid over-funding channels showing declining efficiency.

  • Channel Mix

    Balance acquisition, retention, and brand investments by goal.

    Output: Allocation by objective

    Keep the mix aligned with quarter-level growth priorities.

  • Pacing View

    Track weekly spend pacing to avoid month-end surprises.

    Metric: Spend vs. planned pace

    Adjust mid-month instead of waiting for postmortems.

  • Review Cadence

    Set a weekly review ritual tied to performance dashboards.

    Output: Reallocation checklist

    Keep budgets flexible without drifting from the plan.
