Message checklist

  • Headline matches the ad hook.
  • Value prop is stated in 12 words or less.
  • Primary CTA repeats 3 times.
  • One quantifiable proof point above the fold.

How to use this

  1. Pick one layout and map your current messaging to it.
  2. Test a single section swap before a full redesign.
  3. Track scroll depth and CTA clicks to compare variants.
