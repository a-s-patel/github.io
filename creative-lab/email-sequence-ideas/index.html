Email Sequence Ideas

Lifecycle sequences you can adapt for onboarding, nurture, and retention.

Sequence starters

Day 0: Quick win

Deliver a single action that creates a visible outcome in under 10 minutes.

Day 2: Proof unpack

Share a short case snippet and highlight the one metric that moved.

Day 4: Objection loop

Address the top risk and show how customers avoided it.

Day 7: Upgrade path

Reveal the next feature or workflow that compounds results.

Day 10: Social proof

Curate three wins, each tied to a different persona.

Subject line riffs

  • "Your first win is hiding here."
  • "The 3-minute setup that saves hours."
  • "Proof from a team that started where you are."
  • "Ready for the next level?"

How to use this

  1. Match each email to a product milestone.
  2. Keep one clear CTA per send.
  3. Repurpose replies into new proof cues.
