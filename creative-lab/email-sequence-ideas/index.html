Email Sequence Ideas

Lifecycle sequences with clear triggers, goals, and messaging arcs.

Core sequences

Day 0 welcome

Set expectations, highlight quick wins, drive first action.

Activation sprint

3 emails that remove friction and prove value fast.

Proof drip

Customer story, stat, and social proof over 7 days.

Retention nudge

Usage recap with a single next-step CTA.

Subject line formulas

  • "A quick win you can unlock today"
  • "The step most teams skip"
  • "Your first result is closer than you think"
  • "Proof this works in 10 minutes"

Cadence guide

  1. Day 0: welcome + promise reminder.
  2. Day 2: tutorial or quick setup.
  3. Day 4: proof and results.
  4. Day 7: objection handling + CTA.
  5. Day 10: feature spotlight + upgrade path.

Copy cues

Write in second person, keep paragraphs short, and end each email with one clear action. Use P.S. lines for urgency.
