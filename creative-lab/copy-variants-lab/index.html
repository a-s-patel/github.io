Copy Variants Lab

Messaging matrices to generate fast headline, hook, and CTA experiments.

Variant matrix

Outcome + timeline

"Get [result] in [time] without [common pain]."

Problem + proof

"We fixed [pain] with [proof cue], here is how."

Process + friction

"The 3-step system that removes [friction]."

Persona + promise

"For [persona] who need [promise], not [old way]."

Before/after

"From [old state] to [new state] in one sprint."

CTA variations

  • "Show me the plan."
  • "Get the first win."
  • "See the proof."
  • "Build my first test."

How to use this

  1. Pick one matrix and generate 10 variants.
  2. Swap only the promise while keeping the format.
  3. Promote the top 2 variants into new ads.
