UGC Angles Board

A quick-scan library of hooks, promises, and proof cues for creator-led ads.

Angle library

Problem snap

"I tried everything before this fixed it."

Before/after proof

Show the contrast in 5 seconds, then explain why.

Routine upgrade

Swap one step to unlock a better outcome.

Myth break

Call out a common mistake and offer the fix.

Social proof cut

"Everyone in my group chat uses this now."

Creative prompts

  • Open with a 3-word pain point.
  • Cut to the payoff shot before explaining.
  • Anchor claims to a measurable result.
  • Use a single CTA for the first test.

How to use this board

  1. Pick one promise and one proof cue.
  2. Draft 3 hook variants around the same angle.
  3. Film short cuts and swap only the opening line.

Testing notes

Keep the first batch tight: 5 to 8 variants, one creator, one offer. Label by angle so you can spot winners quickly.
