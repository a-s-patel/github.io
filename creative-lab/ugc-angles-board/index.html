UGC Angles Board

A curated list of hooks, promises, and proof points to remix into short-form UGC ads.

Angle library

Before/after reset

Show the messy reality before and the calm, repeatable outcome after adopting your system.

Founder confession

Open with a mistake you made, then reveal the insight that fixed it.

Myth bust

Call out a common industry belief and explain why your evidence says the opposite.

Proof stack

Lead with a concise data point, then support it with 2-3 qualitative customer quotes.

Speed promise

Highlight a fast win ("in 7 days") and show the first step to get there.

Prompt bank

  • "What surprised you most about the results?"
  • "What did you stop doing once this worked?"
  • "What was the first visible sign it was working?"
  • "What does a typical day look like now?"

How to use this

  1. Pick two angles that match your current offer.
  2. Pair each angle with a proof cue or customer quote.
  3. Write three hook variations per angle.
