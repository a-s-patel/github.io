Ad Creative Scorecards

Use a simple rubric to evaluate clarity, proof, and performance potential before you scale.

Scorecard preview

Rubric cues

  • Hook: outcome is clear in 3 seconds.
  • Proof: one metric or credible testimonial.
  • Structure: beginning, middle, end are distinct.
  • CTA: matches the promise and removes friction.

How to use this

  1. Score concepts before production.
  2. Only scale ads that average 4+ across criteria.
  3. Review the lowest score and rewrite that section.
