Featured Growth Experiment

Email Subject Line A/B Test

By Sarah Johnson, Growth Lead at TechCorp

+32% Open Rate

Increased from 18.5% to 24.4% across 50,000 subscribers

The Hypothesis

Using question-based subject lines with personal pronouns (you/your) will increase open rates for our product announcement emails.

A

"Introducing Our New Analytics Dashboard"

18.5% open rate

B

"Is your marketing data working for you?"

24.4% open rate

Key Learnings

Questions create a curiosity gap that drives opens

Personal pronouns (you/your) create relevance

Problem-focused subjects outperformed feature announcements

Shorter subject lines (under 50 characters) performed best

Day 3-5: Email Creation & Segmentation

Day 6-13: Campaign Run & Data Collection

Day 14: Analysis & Documentation
